### Philosophy
To create a clean, straightforward, and mobile-friendly experience for both local and international clients, recruiters, and headhunters where you can check some of my best works along with my updated CV that is available for both viewing and download.

### Psychology
- **[Color] Neon Green:** Attention-grabber, targets the youth, expresses modernity, visualizing tech and gaming, shows renewed career energy, envisions the future
- **[Tone] Conversational:** Appeals to a wide range of viewers. Casual, but serious. Witty, yet respectful.
- **[Cognitive Load] Low Extraneous Effort:** Simple approach. Site context is clear.

### Anatomy
- **[Header] Fourth Wall:** Conversational, as if I am talking to the viewer, shows personal touch.
- **[Body] Masonry:** Built similar to Pinterest feed shows familiarity and ease of use.
- **[Mobile Nav] Glass:** Modern, elegant, aesthetically pleasing
